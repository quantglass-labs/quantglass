# Backers

Thank you to everyone who sponsors QuantGlass. Your support keeps an honest,
open-source trading **education and research** project independent and moving
forward.

> **What sponsorship is — and is not.** Backing QuantGlass supports open-source
> development and maintenance. It does **not** buy trading signals, investment
> advice, guaranteed outcomes, or preferential information. QuantGlass is
> research and decision-support software, not financial advice — see
> [DISCLAIMER.md](DISCLAIMER.md).

## Become a backer

Use the **Sponsor** button at the top of the
[repository](https://github.com/quantglass-labs/quantglass), or sponsor directly
at [github.com/sponsors/quantglass-labs](https://github.com/sponsors/quantglass-labs).
Recurring or one-time, any amount helps. See [SPONSORS.md](SPONSORS.md) for what
your support funds.
