# Sponsor QuantGlass

QuantGlass is open-source (AGPL-3.0-or-later) trading **education and research**
software. It is built and maintained in the open, and sponsorship keeps that
work going.

> **What sponsorship is — and is not.** Sponsoring QuantGlass supports the
> development and maintenance of open-source software. It does **not** buy
> trading signals, investment advice, guaranteed outcomes, or preferential
> financial information. QuantGlass is research and decision-support software,
> not financial advice — see [DISCLAIMER.md](DISCLAIMER.md).

## Why sponsor

Your support funds:

- Ongoing development of the signal engine, backtesting workbench, paper-trading
  venue, and the learning curriculum.
- Maintenance: bug fixes, dependency and security updates, and documentation.
- Reviewing and merging community contributions and extensions.

## How to sponsor

- **GitHub Sponsors** — use the **Sponsor** button at the top of the
  [repository](https://github.com/quantglass-labs/quantglass) (recurring or
  one-time).

Sponsorship is voluntary support for open-source work. It conveys no ownership,
no control over the roadmap, and no entitlement to financial results. Tiers, if
offered, may include non-financial perks only (for example, acknowledgement in
this file or release notes).

## For companies

If you need terms beyond the AGPL — proprietary embedding, closed-source
redistribution, hosted offerings, or enterprise support — that is a commercial
arrangement rather than a sponsorship. See
[COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md](COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md).
