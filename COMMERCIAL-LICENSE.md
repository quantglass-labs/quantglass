# Commercial Licensing

QuantGlass Community Edition is licensed under AGPL-3.0-or-later.

That license is intentionally open source: anyone may use, study, modify, and
redistribute the software if they comply with the AGPL. Some companies cannot or
do not want to comply with AGPL obligations, especially when embedding the code
inside proprietary products or offering hosted services. For those cases, a
separate commercial license is available.

## When A Commercial License Is Appropriate

A commercial license is intended for:

- Proprietary redistribution or embedding.
- Closed-source forks used in commercial products.
- Hosted or managed services based on QuantGlass.
- Broker, advisory, fund, research-desk, or enterprise deployments that require
  private modifications.
- Paid signal products, strategy marketplaces, or bundled commercial offerings.
- Commercial support, custom integration, or compliance review.

## Possible Commercial Terms

Commercial terms can include one or more of:

- Annual license fee.
- Per-seat or per-desk pricing.
- Revenue share or royalty for paid products built on QuantGlass.
- Custom provider, broker, strategy, or AI integration work.
- Priority support and security response commitments.
- Private deployment or redistribution rights.

## What Stays Free

The AGPL Community Edition remains free for personal use, education, research,
open-source collaboration, and commercial use that complies with AGPL.
