## What Is a Candlestick?

A candlestick is a visual summary of price movement during one selected time period.

| Candle timeframe | What one candle represents |
|---|---|
| 1 minute | All price movement during 1 minute |
| 1 hour | All price movement during 1 hour |
| 1 day | All price movement during 1 trading day |
| 1 week | All price movement during 1 week |

A candle answers four questions:

| Question | Term |
|---|---|
| Where did price start? | Open |
| How high did price go? | High |
| How low did price go? | Low |
| Where did price finish? | Close |

## Key Rule

A candlestick is not a signal by itself. It must be read with trend, support/resistance, volume, and risk.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why dangerous |
|---|---|
| Buying every green candle | Green does not guarantee continuation |
| Selling every red candle | Red does not guarantee continuation |
| Reading one candle alone | Context matters |
| Ignoring volume | Weak participation can weaken the message |
| Ignoring risk | Even good setups can fail |
